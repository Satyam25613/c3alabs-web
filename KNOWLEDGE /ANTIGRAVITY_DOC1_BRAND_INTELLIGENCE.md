## 1. WHAT C3ALABS IS

**C3alabs is a parent company — not a product.**

C3alabs builds AI companies. It currently owns two:
- **KARAX** → Personal AI Workspace (B2C) → lives at karax.ai
- **SARVAX** → AI Agents Platform (B2B) → lives at sarvax.ai

c3alabs.com is the holding company presence. It is NOT a product page. It does NOT sell features. It does NOT explain how KARAX or SARVAX works in detail.

**The analogy:** Alphabet is to Google what C3alabs is to KARAX and SARVAX. The parent brand communicates identity, thesis, and credibility — and points to the companies it has built.

**What c3alabs.com must accomplish:**
1. Make investors want to learn more
2. Make potential hires want to join
3. Make the press understand the category
4. Route the right people to karax.ai or sarvax.ai
5. Make Sai Casula appear as a credible, visionary founder

## 4. BRAND VOICE — PARENT COMPANY

C3alabs.com speaks differently from KARAX.ai and SARVAX.ai.

| KARAX voice | SARVAX voice | C3alabs voice |
|---|---|---|
| Personal, direct, execution-focused | Powerful, ROI-driven, operational | Authoritative, visionary, measured |
| "Just tell KARAX. It handles the rest." | "AI that executes." | "We build AI companies." |
| Speaks to daily pain | Speaks to business cost | Speaks to the future |

### C3alabs Voice Attributes
- **Measured confidence.** Not hype. Statements, not superlatives.
- **Long-term thinking.** We're building something that lasts decades.
- **Sparse.** Every sentence earns its place. No padding.
- **Intelligent.** Assumes the reader is sharp. No over-explanation.
- **Founder-led.** Sai's conviction is present in every word.

### Forbidden on c3alabs.com
- "AI-powered" — says nothing
- "Cutting-edge" — meaningless
- "Leverage" — corporate filler
- "Ecosystem" — overused
- "Seamless" — empty promise
- Any sentence longer than 25 words in a headline context

## 6. THE INVESTOR PAGE PHILOSOPHY

C3alabs does not have investors yet. The Investors page must NOT be a pitch deck. It must NOT beg.

**The approach:** Call the page **"The Thesis."** Frame it as intellectual conviction — a reasoned argument for why this category exists, why now, and why C3alabs is positioned to own it. End with an invitation, not a pitch.

**The effect:** Sophisticated investors read a page like this and reach out. They don't want to be pitched. They want to find founders who are right about something important. Make them feel like they found that.

---

## 7. THE TEAM PAGE PHILOSOPHY

**Only Sai Casula is named.** No fake team members. No placeholder roles presented as if they are filled.

Instead: Sai occupies the top position prominently. Below him: a brief, honest section — "The team is small and growing. We hire for conviction over credentials."

Then: 3–4 open role categories (not specific job posts) to signal what kind of company this is becoming.

This is more credible than a page of placeholder initials.
