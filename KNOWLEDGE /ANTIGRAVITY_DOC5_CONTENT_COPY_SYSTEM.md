## 1. BRAND VOICE RULES — ENFORCED

These rules apply to every word on the site. Antigravity SHALL NOT violate them.

| Rule | Example: Correct | Example: Wrong |
|---|---|---|
| Statements, not superlatives | "We build AI companies." | "We're a leading AI company" |
| Short sentences | "KARAX executes tasks." | "KARAX is a powerful AI-driven platform that enables users to..." |
| No adjective inflation | "Both products are live." | "Two cutting-edge, innovative AI products" |
| Imperative when describing AI | "SARVAX executes your workflows." | "SARVAX helps you manage your workflows" |
| Spare punctuation | One em-dash max per sentence | Multiple commas, semicolons, parentheses |
| Founder voice in first person | "We started with conviction." | "C3alabs was founded with the belief..." |
| No filler softeners | "This is the beginning." | "We're excited to be on this journey" |

**Forbidden words on c3alabs.com:**
- AI-powered
- Cutting-edge
- Leverage (as a verb)
- Ecosystem
- Seamless
- Next-generation (unless in a very specific headline)
- Game-changer / Revolutionary
- Excited / Thrilled / Honored
- Solutions / Platform (avoid — use specific words instead)
- Unlock / Empower / Transform

### The Argument
```
01 / The problem with AI today

Every major AI product launched in the last three years is built around
the same interaction model: you type, it replies. The value is in the response.
The work still falls on you.

This is not a coincidence. It is the path of least resistance.
Building AI that generates is far easier than building AI that executes.

---

02 / The shift that's coming

The next phase of AI adoption is not about better models.
It's about AI that owns workflows.

The companies that will define the next decade are not building
better chatbots. They're building systems where the AI does the job —
completely, accountably, and autonomously.

---

03 / Why two products

The execution problem looks different at the individual level than at the
business level. These are not the same market, the same buyer, or the same motion.

KARAX is built for the individual who wants to multiply what they can do alone.
SARVAX is built for the business that wants to operate without adding headcount.

Same infrastructure. Different surfaces. Dual-market coverage.

---

04 / Why now

Model capability crossed a threshold in 2023. The question is no longer
whether AI can do the work. It's whether anyone is building the right
interface around it.

Most companies are racing to build better models. We're building the layer
on top — the one that converts capability into execution. That layer is
largely unbuilt.

---

05 / Why C3alabs

We started with conviction, not capital. Both products are live.
Both have users. Both are growing.

We are not fundraising to prove the idea. We are fundraising to accelerate
what is already working.
```

## 11. TYPOGRAPHY RULES FOR COPY RENDERING

These rules govern how body copy is formatted in code.

1. **Long em-dash (—):** Used as a sentence break. Rendered as `&mdash;` in HTML or `—` directly in JSX. No spaces before or after in headlines. One space before and after in body text.

2. **Italics in headlines:** Use `<em>` tag. It renders as Fraunces italic. Maximum one italic word per headline.

3. **Line breaks in headlines:** Use `<br />` in JSX for precise editorial breaks. Never let the browser wrap headlines automatically.

4. **Widow prevention:** Last word of a paragraph should not be a single word on its own line. Use `&nbsp;` between the last two words if needed.

5. **Quotation marks:** Use typographic quotes `"` `"` — never straight quotes `"` `"`.

6. **Ellipsis:** Use `…` (single character), never three periods `...`.

7. **Numbers in stats:** Always in Fraunces 300. Sub-labels in DM Mono. Never swap.
